So, what about me?

Hi there! I come from Sevilla, south of Spain. There, I studied Humanities degree and Spanish as foreigners Bachelor. After some years, I couln't find a stability while I felt disconected and dissatisfied with myself and about what I was doing. I decided to challenge myself, get out of my comfort zone and be brave. I moved to Stockholm (Sweden) at the beginning of 2021. Since then, I knew new friends, places, and a wide range of points of view, and with that in my pocket, I encouraged myself to find what I truly wished to be and to find a profession that really provokes me that curiosity and interest of improving day by day. That one with which you lose the perception of time because you love it! And here I am, rising from my ashes and finding my place as a frontend developer. There I go!
